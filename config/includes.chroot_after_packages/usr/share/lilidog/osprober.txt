                       
                   OSprober Toggle
                        
    OSprober is a utility that works in conjuntion 
    with grub to provide a list at boot of all the 
    operating systems listed on your computor.   
    
    Use the Toggle button to enable osprober on or 
    off. The log is in /var/log/grub-update.log
    
    Please wait for grub to finish updating before 
    rebooting. Look for the progress update dialog
    to disappear upon completion.
